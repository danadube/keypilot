# KeyPilot Project Context

## Product
KeyPilot is a real estate operations platform.

## Current focus
ShowingHQ is the first major product surface and should feel production-ready.

## Module intent
- ShowingHQ: private showings, open houses, visitors, follow-ups, host workflows, Supra-related showing intake
- PropertyVault: property assets, photos, flyers, listing materials
- Transactions: pipeline, commission tracking, closings
- ClientKeep: CRM and lead/contact lifecycle
- FarmTrackr: geographic and farming workflows
- SellerPulse: seller reporting and feedback summaries
- MarketPilot: marketing and campaign automation

## Key workflow distinctions
### Showings
Private scheduled appointments.
May come from Supra or manual entry.
Typically need feedback and follow-up.

### Open Houses
Public events.
Use QR sign-in, visitor capture, flyer delivery, host workflows, and open-house reporting.

## Product rules
- never blur showings and open houses
- public pages should not feel like admin pages
- host workflows should be simple and device-friendly
- dashboard should emphasize today's work and lead conversion
