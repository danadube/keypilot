## Phase 1 – Open House MVP

Core functionality:

- Property management
- Open house event creation
- Tablet sign-in system
- QR sign-in for visitors
- Lead capture
- Contact creation
- Activity timeline
- Follow-up draft generation
- Seller summary reports
- PDF export

Goal: Launch a usable product for real estate agents.

---

## Phase 2 – CRM Layer

Add:

- ClientKeep (contact CRM)
- communication timeline
- follow-up automation
- relationship tracking

---

## Phase 3 – Transaction System

Add:

- DealForge (deal pipeline)
- OfferScope (offer comparison)
- CloseLine (transaction management)

---

## Phase 4 – Intelligence Layer

Add:

- FarmTrackr (farming intelligence)
- OpportunityRadar (listing predictions)
- InsightDeck (analytics dashboards)

---

## Phase 5 – Automation

Add:

- AutoPilot workflow engine
- automated follow-ups
- automated listing alerts
- AI-driven lead prioritization

---

## Long-Term Vision

KeyPilot becomes a **complete real estate business operating system**.

Agents should be able to run their entire workflow from one platform:

Prospecting → Lead Capture → Showings → Deals → Transactions → Analytics → Automation
